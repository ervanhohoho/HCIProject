Help us reach our goals

Duchenne Muscular Dystrophy (DMD) is a mutation in the DNA that produces the protein dystrophin. The DNA of children with DMD have a deletion, duplication, or an abrupt stop in the DNA-protein production process.

Regardless of the amount, every donation makes a big difference to children living with with DMD. All money raised will fund DMD treatment research and help us raise awareness through local and national events. The research represents a potentially major breakthrough in the treatment of DMD which could lead to longer life expectancy and a better overall quality of life. By supporting our events you will help us educate the world about the dangers of this crippling disease and give us a platform to show that when we come together, we can put an end to DMD.
